Purpose

HTML5 tags that gives meaning to the document for screen readers, computer programs and search engines

Semantic elements

The main content of a page. Only one tag per page. Can help screen reader and for keyboard commands.
An independent item like: A forum post, an article, a blog entry, a comment, a news article, an interactive widget.
Sidebars. Content that is related to the main content. Not critical for the main content.
Thematic grouping of content or topic. Includes header Every Article or Section talks about one topic only and defines it in depth. Often Article is being understood as a wider term whereas Section is understood as a narrower one. Sections are often the sub-divisions of the Articles and collectively form Article. Therefore, Articles are the combination of Sections.
A group of introductory or navigational aids. Heading elements, logo, searchbar, navigation. May contain serveral h-tags (h1, h2, h3...)
Who wrote the content. Links to releated documents. Copyright. Contact info. Sociala media links.
A section with navigation links. The main navigation (e.g in the top or at the side) En screen reader may decide to skip this and go straight to the main content
